About Politics

Politics is the process of making decisions that apply to members of a group. It involves the activities associated with governance and public administration. Political systems and ideologies vary widely across different societies.

Engaging in politics allows individuals to have a voice in shaping their communities, influencing policies, and participating in democratic processes.

The Right to Vote

The right to vote, also known as suffrage, is a fundamental human right in democratic societies. It allows eligible citizens to participate in choosing their representatives and shaping the direction of their government.

Voting ensures that leaders are accountable to the people they serve and that diverse perspectives are represented in decision-making processes.
